THE MAKING OF A KING HRH Igwe Engr Jude Onyenwe Eze was born as the first son and second child to the family of Ozo Prince Christopher Eze (London boy) of Amenu Edem and Mrs Theresa Anyaburu Eze from Akpa Edem in 1966. Prince Christopher was the second son of the then reigning king of Edem, Eze Ngwoke Eze. Young Onyenwe started his primary education at Onuegu Mission, the present day CPS Ugwu na Agbo Community primary school in September 1974. His siblings are Victoria, Stella, Nwamaka , Okwudili, Lebechi, Nkechi and Ifeanacho. Like all great men, HRH Engr Jude O. Eze had his shaky start when in 1976, he underwent a major surgery due to ruptured appendicitis at Akulue Memorial Hospital Onuiyi, Nsukka. This exercise nearly took his life due to series of complications. He survived and emerged from the event with strong will and hard boiled skin to navigate the onerous challenges of life.
